About Me

I am a final-year undergraduate student studying for a BSc in Bioinformatics. As an emerging enthusiastic academician with a polymath orientation, my primary focus is on applying AI and Machine Learning to Biology, specifically exploring forward and inverse protein folding using tools like AlphaFold and PiFold.

Beyond academics, I’m constantly exploring new concepts, experimenting with code, and thinking about future-focused solutions, from AI in genomics to space science and emerging technologies. I value learning across disciplines and pushing boundaries to better understand both the natural world and the systems we build around it.
